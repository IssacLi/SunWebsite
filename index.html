About Me

I hold two Ph.D. degrees from the Department of Computer Science, Virginia Tech and from the School of Telecommunications, Xidian University, respectively. My research covers various topics in cybersecurity with the focus on security and privacy in networked information systems, cyber-physical systems and decentralized systems, including Cloud computing, Internet of Things, Blockchains, computer/mobile systems, and wireless network.
