Short Bio

I hold two Ph.D. degrees from the Department of Computer Science, Virginia Tech and from the School of Telecommunications, Xidian University, respectively. My research covers various topics in cybersecurity with the focus on data security and privacy, decentralized trust and infrastructure, cyber-physical system and applied cryptography.
