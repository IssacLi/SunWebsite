Opportunities


I am looking for self-motivated students who are interested in pursuing Ph.D. degrees in the network and computer security at Purdue University – West Lafayette, USA. Financial support will be provided based on the eligibility of the applicants.

Prospective students should choose to enroll in the Department of Computer Information and Technology. Students who meet the following requirements are encouraged to apply:

  • B.S. or M.S. degree in CS, EE or a relevant area (Students with B.S. degrees should apply for our direct-Ph.D. program.)

  • Excellent academic record

  • Excellent communication and writing skills

  • Some prior research experiences in Blockchain, Internet of Things, AI, wireless networks, distributed network/system, embedded systems, mobile computing, programming languages, and operating systems, are preferable

  • Background in computer/network security or cryptography is a plus, but not necessary

Please send your CV, academic transcripts, GRE/TOEFL scores, and other supporting documents as ONE PDF attachment to me.

Visiting/exchange scholars and students who have matching interests and strong research backgrounds are also welcome to contact me.
